# Job Registry

One row per job. Full JD lives in `data/jobs/details/<id>.md`; evaluations in
`data/results/<id>.md`.

- `id` — stable slug `company-title`.
- `jd_hash` — first 8 chars of sha1 of the detail file; re-scored when it changes.
- `status` — application state (mirrors your board).
